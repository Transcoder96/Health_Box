Cycle Science

Your 4 Cycle Phases Explained

Your body goes through 4 distinct phases every month — each with unique energy, mood, and nutritional needs.

🩸 Menstrual (Day 1–5) 🌱 Follicular (Day 6–13) 🌟 Ovulation (Day 14) 🌙 Luteal (Day 15–28)
💡 Tip: Eat iron-rich foods in menstrual phase, carbs in luteal phase for energy & mood balance.
🥗

Vitals

Understanding Your Vitals

Your sensor tracks three critical markers that together tell a complete picture of your health.

❤️
Heart Rate
Normal: 60–100 BPM. Above 110 may signal stress or anaemia.
🩸
Blood Oxygen (SpO2)
Normal: 95–100%. Below 94% can indicate anaemia or low iron.
🌡️
Body Temperature
Normal: 36.1–37.5°C. Spikes may signal infection or ovulation.
💪
Exercise

Move With Your Cycle

Working out in sync with your hormones boosts results and reduces pain. Not all days are the same!

🩸 Day 1–5 (Menstrual) Rest · Light yoga · Walking
🌱 Day 6–13 (Follicular) Strength · Cardio · Dance
🌟 Day 14 (Ovulation) HIIT · Running · Sports
🌙 Day 15–28 (Luteal) Pilates · Swimming · Stretching
🧠
Mental Health

Managing PMS & Mood

Hormonal changes affect your brain chemistry. Here's how to ride the wave with grace.

😴 Sleep 7–8 hrs — low sleep worsens cramps and mood 3×
☀️ Get 15 min of sunlight daily to boost serotonin naturally
🧘 Meditation 10 min/day reduces cortisol by up to 30%
📓 Journaling your feelings reduces emotional intensity
🚫 Avoid alcohol — it depletes B-vitamins that regulate mood
💡 Remember: Mood swings 5–7 days before your period are PMS — log them in your daily diary.
🔴
Anaemia Awareness

Iron Deficiency & Women

1 in 3 Indian women are anaemic. Heavy periods, low SpO2, and fatigue are the key warning signs.

🚨 Warning Signs of Anaemia:
Pale skin · Extreme tiredness · SpO2 below 94% · Fast heartbeat · Dizziness · Breathlessness

Hydration

Water & Women's Health

Dehydration worsens cramps, fatigue, and brain fog. Water is your cheapest medicine.

🥤
Target: 8–10 glasses/day
Increases to 12 glasses during periods
☕ Replace 1 coffee with herbal tea (ginger, chamomile)
🍉 Eat water-rich foods: cucumber, watermelon, curd
⏰ Drink a glass of water right after waking up
🧂 Add a pinch of rock salt + lemon if feeling tired
